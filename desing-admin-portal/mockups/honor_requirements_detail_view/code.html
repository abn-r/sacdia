First Aid - Basic

This honor introduces fundamental life-saving techniques. Participants will learn how to assess emergency situations, perform CPR, and treat common injuries.

list_alt Requirements Checklist

12 Steps Total

Section 1: General Principles

check

1. Know the basic principles of mouth-to-mouth breathing.

Include the proper head tilt, checking for obstructions, and the rate of compressions vs breaths for adults and infants.

2. Know how to control bleeding.

Demonstrate direct pressure, elevation, and pressure points. Understand when to use a tourniquet as a last resort.

Section 2: Emergency Response

3. Know the procedure for treating a victim of poisoning.

Instructor Note: Emphasize contacting Poison Control immediately. Do not induce vomiting unless instructed.

4. Demonstrate the proper procedure for splinting broken bones.

Perform splinting on:

  • Lower arm
  • Upper arm
  • Lower leg

5. Know the proper procedure to help a choking victim.

Demonstrate the Heimlich maneuver on a standing and lying victim.

Section 3: Practical Exam

6. Complete a mock disaster scenario.

Participate in a triage simulation with at least 3 types of injuries represented.
